Instructions

- Click the extension icon to copy the URL of the current tab.

- To copy the URL of a different tab: Right-click on a tab in the sidebar and select "Copy Tab URL".

- To add the icon to your toolbar:

  1. Click on the extensions dropdown on the toolbar.
  2. Right-click the "Copy Tab URL" extension and choose "Pin to Toolbar".
  3. (Optional) To move the icon to a different position, right-click the toolbar and choose "Customize Toolbar". Then drag the icon wherever you want.
